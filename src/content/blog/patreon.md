Hey everyone!

For a long time now, I've wanted a way to truly give back to the amazing people who have been asking how they can support my music on a monthly basis.

I pour my heart and soul into every rock and acoustic cover, and your encouragement means the world to me. That's why I'm so excited to announce that I've officially launched my Patreon page! It's a space for those who want to go beyond the YouTube versions and officially join my (hopefully growing community xD). This is my way of saying a massive thank you and providing something special in return for helping me keep producing music consistently and independently.

Depending on how you choose to support the channel, I’ve put together some awesome rewards for you to experience the music beyond just listening. You'll be able to get early access to my new releases, high-quality MP3 downloads of all my covers, and even the official karaoke and backing tracks if you want to sing or play along! For those who want to take that extra step to help make these productions possible, there's even a tier where your name will be featured at the end of my videos as an Executive Producer.

If you want to step inside the studio with me and be a foundational part of this journey, head over to [my Patreon page](https://www.patreon.com/cw/tallescattarin) to check out the tiers. Thank you so much for believing in this project and being part of the sound! 🤘🏻
