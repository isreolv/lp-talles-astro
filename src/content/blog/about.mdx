Hey everyone! I'm Talles, a musician, singer, and audio engineer based in Curitiba, Brazil.

If I had to sum up my life in one sentence, it would be this: music is the air that I breathe. My journey started really early—around three years old, to be exact! Growing up, my house was always filled with melodies, largely because my mom is a pianist. My parents noticed I was hooked from day one, so they fueled that fire by giving me a recorder and a kid's drum kit. By the time I turned 10, I got my first real guitar, and there was no looking back. For me, music has never been just a profession; it’s a deep emotional connection, a true lifestyle, and the absolute best way I know how to express myself.

### Songs You Know in a Way You've Never Heard Before
As I grew as an artist, I wanted to share this passion with the world, which led me to start my YouTube channel.
I love taking the songs that marked entire generations—and my own life—and flipping them on their heads. My goal is always to deliver songs you know in a way you've never heard before, breathing a fresh rock or acoustic soul into familiar classics. There's something incredibly special about finding a new emotional core inside a melody that people already love.

### 10+ Years of Crafting Sound
Over the past decade, I've poured my heart into every aspect of track creation, from arranging and recording to the final mix. Now, with more than 10 years of experience under my belt, I've opened up my studio to help other artists achieve their vision. Whether I'm producing a track from scratch, mixing and mastering your stems, or laying down lead vocals, I bring that same lifelong passion to every project I touch.
