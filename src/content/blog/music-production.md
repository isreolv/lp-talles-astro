What's up, everyone? Greetings from Curitiba! 🎸

If you've spent any time on my YouTube channel, you know my absolute favorite thing in the world is taking a well-known song, stripping it down to its core, and rebuilding it with a fresh rock or acoustic vibe. There is something magical about finding a new soul inside a familiar melody.

But over the years, spending countless hours in my studio crafting those covers, I've realized something else I'm deeply passionate about: **Music Production**. 

I've had so many local artists and online friends ask me how I get my tracks to sound the way they do, and the truth is, I love the *process* of building a song just as much as I love playing it. That's why I'm officially opening up my studio doors (virtually and physically) to offer my services as a Music Producer!

Here is how we can work together to bring your musical vision to life:

### 1. Full-Service Production (I Record for You)
Do you have a voice memo of a killer melody? A lyric sheet and a basic chord progression? Send it my way. We will collaborate to find the perfect arrangement. I can lay down the acoustic and electric guitars, bass, program or record the drums, and build out the entire instrumental track from the ground up. You just provide the vision (your vocals! _or mine_), and I provide the band.

### 2. Co-Production (Send Me Your Tracks)
Maybe you already have a session going. You've recorded your guitars, synths, and vocals, but the track feels like it's missing that final *push*. Send me your stems! I can add new layers, refine your arrangements, re-amp your guitars, or just provide that objective producer's ear to organize and elevate what you've already recorded.
